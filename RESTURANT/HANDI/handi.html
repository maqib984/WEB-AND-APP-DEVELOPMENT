HANDI

Handi is a traditional Indian dish that delights the senses with its aromatic flavors and vibrant colors. This culinary masterpiece is a fragrant blend of spices, tender meat or vegetables, and rich gravy. The dish is typically slow-cooked in a handi, a traditional earthenware pot, which imparts a unique earthy taste to the food. Handi is a true representation of the rich culinary heritage of India, offering a delightful combination of flavors and textures that leave a lasting impression on anyone fortunate enough to savor its delectable taste.
